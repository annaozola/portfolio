## Summary

<Grid2>

KROKS is a Latvian architecture firm, founded in 1992., with an extensive building design and historical building reconstruction portfolio. One of their most known projects being the Riga Castle interior design.

This project was created as my final project during my last year studying at Riga Art and Media School. There were multiple steps taken in order to create the website such as gathering information, research, wireframing, design creation, prototyping, logo and icon design, photo editing and web design.

## Logo redesign

<PSpacing2>

When redesigning the Kroks logo, I wanted to redesign the logo to be more modern while keeping it's initial qualities such as the well-established blue horizon color. I knew the logo had to be able to capture attention and stay memorizable, so I picked a bold font that stands out - Work Sans Bold. This logo design variation was selected as final.

</PSpacing2>

</PSpacing>
